Personality

I  don't like anything that is obvious , no matter what it is ― movie, song or even a joke. Everything should trigger mental activity. Therefore,  I love to solve tasks  and for me, the best gift is a puzzle or a book. For over  13 years, my first and only love, who eventually became  my wife, is the biggest  inspiration of my life.
